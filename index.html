Company Description


Steels utensils was established in 1974 and is the pioneer of Stainless Steel Utensils and Aluminium cookware’s in India. Over past 45 years the company has expanded its portfolio of Products to include a wide range of end home solutions.


History Of Our Steel Utensils

The Steel utensils was founded at delhi in 1970 which began its production in 1974. As per the record till 2020 we have more than 10 lakhs satisfied customer . we have more than 40 years for experience in this feild. The company launched a major modernization and expansion program in 2001.
